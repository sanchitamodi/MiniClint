# MiniClint

AI Summer Camp 2025 at Cal Poly San Luis Obispo (AWS and DxHub)

HACKATHON CHALLENGE
TEAM 6: Math Placement 

Overview:
Mini Clint is a AI chatbot designed to assist incoming students with math placement and course planning. The objective was to recuce the amount of calls and emails recieved by Cal Poly's Math Department regarding math placement. The tool integrates with student records to provide personalized guidance based on their records, and respond to common questions.

Objectives: 
- Develop an algorithm for placing students in courses.
- Create a chatbot to answer student question and provide action plans.
- Link the chatbot to student records such as AP and SAT scores for more personalized guidance.
- Reduce student anxiety and staff workload by automating responses to frequent inquiries.
